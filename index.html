Our Mission

World Won't Stop initiative is a global relief effort that aims to provide respite for all the daily wage workers in poor countries affected by the COVID-19 outbreak. We aim to provide a global index of street vendors available to everyone by the end of April 2020.

COVID-19 Common Myths, BUSTED!

This is completely false, there has been no evidence on getting infected by meat or food of any kind.

Although there stands a risk of getting infected if you come into contact with someone infected at a restaurant, food outlets are taking all preventive measures to secure themselves and their delivery personnel.

We will have every registered person on our index to go through a COVID-19 test and only sanction them if they test negative. As a precaution, delivery personnel will have access to masks and sanitizers at factory prices to ensure a safe delivery.
